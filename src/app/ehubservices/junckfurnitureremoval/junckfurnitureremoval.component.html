Junk and Furniture removal

We are happy to help you get rid of unwanted items – large or small.

If it’s taking up space and you don’t want it, we’ll take it off your hands! We offer disposal services for homes, offices and retail properties. When you use our disposal services, you’re also helping the environment because we will recycle or donate items wherever possible.

One of the places we recommend is BIG Reuse, formally Build It Green Toronto!

Why Choose Our Disposal Services?

  1. Your moving crew will take away items on the day of the move. No need for additional scheduling or making separate arrangements
  2. Up front pricing—When we arrive, we’ll take a look at what you want removed and provide you with an all-inclusive price.
  3. Responsible disposal—We’ll do our best to donate and recycle as much as we can – keeping items out of the landfill!

Moving Services

We are responsive to online estimate requests, during which process questions are answered timely. Once a job is booked, you decide what level of valuation is appropriate for the move, after which all building issues are addressed, including issuance of Certificates of Insurance (COIs), deadlines, elevator windows, floor protection requirements, etc. Finally, the move is put on our schedule.
